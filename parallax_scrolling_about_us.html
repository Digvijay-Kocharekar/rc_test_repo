What is ROTARACT

Rotaract is a service club for young people ages 18â€“30 that's sponsored by Rotary International. Rotaract clubs are usually based at universities or in communities, and are part of a global effort to promote international understanding and peace. The clubs' goals are to help young adults develop their skills and knowledge, and to address the social and physical needs of their communities. Rotaract clubs are non-discriminatory and non-political, and anyone can join, regardless of gender, creed, or caste.

Rotaract clubs focus on community service, professional development, and leadership development. Club members exchange ideas with community leaders, and have fun through service.

What is District 3142

Rotaract District 3142 focuses on the development of young adults as leaders in their communities, institutes and workplaces. The purpose of Rotaract is to provide an opportunity for young men and women to enhance the knowledge and skills that will assist them in personal development, to address the physical and social needs of their communities, and to promote better relations between all people worldwide through a framework of friendship and service.

Rotaract District 3142 spans over the geographical areas of Navi mumbai, Thane, Bhiwandi, Kalyan, Dombivli, Ambernath, Ulhasnagar & under lying areas. It has over 54 Rotaract clubs across these areas with over 3000+ active Rotaract members consisting of both college based as well as community based clubs.

Avenues in Rotaract

The avenues of service include Community Service, Entrepreneurship Development, Professional Development, International Service, Club Service and Sports Development. The district also has support functions which include Editorials, Digital Communications, Public Relations and Marketing.
